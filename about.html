Meet Our Experts

Dr. N. Seshaiah M.S.

Chairman & Managing Director, Senior General Surgeon & Laparoscopic Surgeon, Ex. Professor & Head, Dept. of Surgery, Govt. General Hospital, Guntur

Biography

Dr. Nimmala Seshaiah was born into an agricultural family in the rural area of Nellore district, Andhra Pradesh. His early life was defined by perseverance, as he walked 7 kilometers daily to attend high school at ZPH School, Thotapalligudur, after completing his elementary education in Koverapallem village. He later pursued his intermediate studies at VR College, Nellore, and secured an MBBS seat at SV Medical College, Tirupati, on his first attempt.

Dr. Seshaiah began his medical career as a Factory Medical Officer at Cominco Binani Zinc Factory in Kerala. In 1982, he transitioned to government service, furthering his education by completing an MS in General Surgery at Rangaraya Medical College, Kakinada. He joined GGH Guntur as an Assistant Professor, eventually becoming the Head of the General Surgery Department. His commitment to teaching and mentoring has shaped many future doctors.

In addition to his academic and professional contributions, Dr. Seshaiah has had a lasting impact on the community. On August 15, 1988, he founded Dr.Seshaiah Prajavydyasala Multispeciality Hospital as a small clinic, charging just 5 rupees for patient consultations to ensure that healthcare was accessible to all. Through hard work and dedication, he expanded the clinic into a full-fledged multispeciality hospital by 1995, providing high-quality medical and surgical treatments at affordable prices, particularly for the poor and needy.

Dr. Seshaiah’s lifelong mission has been to serve the underserved, and his work continues to bring vital healthcare services to those who need it most. His journey from humble beginnings to becoming a leader in the medical field is an inspiring story of dedication, compassion, and community service.

Dr. G. Saila Bala M.D.

Ex. Principal & Superintendent, Guntur Medical College, Govt. General Hospital, Guntur

Biography

Dr. G.Sailabala is a distinguished medical professional with an impressive 38-year career in government service. She has held several key positions, including Head of the Pathology Department, Principal of Guntur Medical College, and Superintendent of Guntur Government Hospital. With over 32 years of teaching experience, Dr. Sailabala has played a crucial role in shaping the careers of countless medical professionals, contributing significantly to the healthcare community by mentoring and guiding future doctors.

Throughout her tenure in academia and hospital administration, Dr. Sailabala was known for her leadership and dedication to improving medical education and patient care. Her efforts have helped produce many successful doctors who are now serving society with the skills and knowledge imparted by her.

After her retirement from government service, Dr. Sailabala joined her husband, Dr. Nimmala Seshaiah, at Dr.Seshaiah Prajavydyasala Multispeciality Hospital. In this role, she efficiently manages the hospital’s operations, ensuring that it continues to provide high-quality medical care to the community, particularly for the underserved and needy.

Dr. Sailabala’s lifelong dedication to medicine, education, and healthcare management has left an indelible mark on the medical field, and she continues to make a meaningful difference in her role at the hospital.

Dr. N. Pavan Kumar M.S., MCh.

General & Laparoscopic Surgeon, Pediatric Surgeon & Urologist

Biography

Dr. Pavan Kumar Nimmala is a distinguished surgeon specializing in adult, neonatal, and pediatric surgical care, with additional expertise in minimally invasive laparoscopic techniques and laser proctology surgery. He is dedicated to providing top-tier surgical care to the most vulnerable patients, ensuring precise and compassionate treatment.

Dr. Pavan Kumar Nimmala began his medical journey with an MBBS from Bharati Vidyapeeth Medical College in Pune, followed by an MS in General Surgery from Kamineni Institute of Medical Sciences. His pursuit of surgical excellence led him to achieve an MCh from the prestigious Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education and Research (JIPMER) in Puducherry, where he specialized in complex neonatal and pediatric surgeries.

In addition to his advanced surgical expertise, Dr. Pavan Kumar Nimmala has now joined forces with his father, Dr. Nimmala Seshaiah, a renowned surgeon, at Dr.Seshaiah Prajavydyasala Multispeciality Hospital. Together, they are committed to serving the poor and needy, offering world-class care to underserved communities. Through their collaborative efforts, the hospital continues to be a beacon of hope, providing high-quality, affordable healthcare to those in need.

Dr. Pavan Kumar Nimmala’s deep compassion and expertise reflect his dedication not only to advancing medical care but also to making a meaningful impact on the lives of patients who may otherwise lack access to critical treatments.

Dr. N. Sindhura M.B.B.S., D.C.D.

Fellowship in Cosmetic Dermatology (UK)

Biography

Dr. Sindhura.Y is an experienced dermatologist and Aesthetic Physician with 13 years of expertise in the field of aesthetic medicine and cosmetology. She earned her MBBS from Bharati Vidyapeeth Medical College and completed a Diploma in Clinical Dermatology at Heath Hospital, Cardiff (UK). Dr. Sindhura further advanced her skills with specialized training in aesthetic medicine at Wigmore Medical, Central London.

Dr. Sindhura specializes in a range of cosmetic skin procedures like acne treatment, acne scar revisions (peels and lasers), skin brightening and lightening treatments, Tattoo removal, as well as anti-aging treatments such as Botox, dermal fillers, and lip fillers. Additionally, she has substantial experience in hair regrowth treatments like PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma), GFC (Growth Factor Concentrate), and hair transplants. Her comprehensive knowledge and expertise in aesthetic medicine enable her to offer customized treatments tailored to each patient’s needs, enhancing both skin health and appearance.

Dr. S. Siva Sankaraiah M.S. Ortho (NIMS, Hyd), MCh.

Orthopedic Surgeon, Joint Replacement Surgeon & Sports Injury Specialist

Biography

Dr. S. Sivasankaraiah, M.S. Ortho., is an accomplished orthopedic surgeon with over 13 years of extensive experience in the field. He completed his medical training at the prestigious Nizam’s Institute of Medical Sciences (NIMS) in Hyderabad, where he specialized in orthopedics. Additionally, he pursued a Fellowship in Joint Replacement (FIJR) at Sunshine Hospital, Hyderabad, where he gained advanced expertise in joint replacement surgeries.

Dr. Sivasankaraiah has successfully performed over 3,000 joint replacement surgeries, with a particular emphasis on therapy-free fast track knee replacement surgeries, a cutting-edge approach that enhances recovery and reduces the need for prolonged therapy. He is well-versed in handling complex trauma cases, cold orthopedics, and orthopedic oncology, having conducted thousands of complex trauma surgeries with outstanding results.

His areas of specialization include:

  • Orthopedic Joint Replacement
  • Orthopedic Complex Trauma
  • Orthopaedic Spine Problems/Surgeries
  • Cold Orthopedics & Orthopedic Oncology
  • Orthopedic Sports Injuries
  • Orthopedic Chronic Pain Management

At present, Dr. Sivasankaraiah has joined forces with his father-in-law, Dr. Nimmala Seshaiah, a renowned surgeon, at Dr. Seshaiah Prajavydyasala Multispeciality Hospital. Together, they are dedicated to providing high-quality healthcare to the underprivileged, focusing on serving the poor and needy with affordable, world-class medical care.

Through his extensive experience and compassionate approach, Dr. Sivasankaraiah continues to make a significant impact in the field of orthopedics, particularly in joint replacement and trauma care, while contributing to the betterment of society by offering his skills to those in need.

Dr. N. Niharika M.S. FMAS

Obstetrician, Gynecologist & Laparoscopic Surgeon

Biography

Dr. Niharika is a highly skilled obstetrician and gynecologist with extensive training in both laparoscopic and open surgeries. She earned her MBBS from NRI Medical College and went on to complete her MS in Obstetrics and Gynecology at Sri Devaraj Urs Medical College. Driven by a passion for minimally invasive surgery, Dr. Niharika pursued specialized training and a fellowship in laparoscopic surgery at the renowned World Laparoscopy Hospital in Gurgaon, Delhi.

Throughout her career, Dr. Niharika has gained significant expertise in performing complex laparoscopic and open hysterectomies, along with managing high-risk pregnancies. Her tenure as a senior resident at Guntur Medical College allowed her to further refine her surgical skills and deepen her knowledge in women’s health.

In addition to her formal training, Dr. Niharika has drawn invaluable experience from working alongside her father, Dr. Nimmala Seshaiah, a highly respected surgeon. Currently, she is contributing her expertise at Dr.Seshaiah Prajavydyasala Multispeciality Hospital, where she works closely with her father, offering high-quality medical care to patients in need.

Dr. Niharika’s dedication to women’s health, her advanced surgical skills, and her commitment to serving the community make her a trusted and compassionate healthcare provider.
