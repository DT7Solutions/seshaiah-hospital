Welcome to the Surgical Gastroenterology Department at Dr. Seshaiah Prajavydyasala Hospital

In our gastroenterology department, our surgical gastroenterologists in Guntur specialize in the surgical management of diseases and disorders affecting the gastrointestinal (GI) tract. We offer expert care through advanced methods providing high-quality care to help patients achieve optimal digestive health.

What is Surgical Gastroenterology?

Surgical gastroenterology focuses on gentle, expert care for your digestive health. Our specialists treat everything from stomach to pancreas issues, using advanced techniques to help you heal and feel your best.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of surgical services, including:

  • Laparoscopic Surgery: Minimally invasive procedures for conditions like gallstones, hernias, and colorectal diseases, resulting in reduced recovery times and less postoperative pain.
  • Bariatric Surgery: Surgical options for weight loss, including gastric bypass and sleeve gastrectomy, to help individuals with obesity achieve significant health improvements.
  • Colorectal Surgery: Management of colorectal diseases, including cancer and inflammatory bowel disease (IBD), with resections, stoma creation, and other necessary procedures.
  • Esophageal Surgery: Treatments for conditions like esophageal cancer and GERD, utilizing both open and minimally invasive techniques.
  • Hepatobiliary Surgery: Surgical care for liver and biliary tract diseases, including liver resections and gallbladder removal.

Advanced Diagnostic Services

Accurate diagnosis is essential for effective treatment. Therefore, our gastroenterology treatment uses advanced diagnostic tools, including:

  • Endoscopy: Procedures to visualize the GI tract, allowing for biopsy and treatment of conditions like bleeding or strictures.
  • Imaging Studies: Utilizing CT scans, MRIs, and ultrasounds to provide detailed images of the digestive organs for accurate assessment.
  • Lab Tests: Comprehensive laboratory evaluations to assess liver function, nutritional status, and other relevant parameters.

Treatment Approaches

Your care, your way: tailored treatment plans designed just for you. Options may include:

  • Medication Management: First we suggest and resort to prescribing medications to manage symptoms, control inflammation, or prepare for surgery.
  • Minimally Invasive Techniques: We understand that surgery is a bit daunting for everyone. Therefore, our expert gastroenterologists in Guntur employ the latest surgical methods to minimize recovery time and complications.
  • Nutritional Support: We also offer full guidance on dietary changes to optimize health before and after surgery.

Patient Education and Resources

We believe that informed patients are better equipped to make decisions about their care. Our educational resources include:

  • FAQs: Answers to common questions regarding surgical gastroenterology procedures and recovery.
  • Pre- and Post-Operative Guidelines: Essential information to help you prepare for surgery and manage your recovery effectively.
  • Support Resources: Information on connecting with support groups and community resources for ongoing care and education.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to schedule a consultation with our surgical gastroenterologists in Guntur, please don’t hesitate to reach out. Our dedicated team is here to provide expert surgical gastroenterology care, helping you achieve better digestive health and quality of life.
