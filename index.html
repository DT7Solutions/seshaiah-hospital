About Us

Dr. Seshaiah's
Praja Vydyasala

Welcome to Dr. Seshaiah's Praja Vydyasala, a trusted multispeciality hospital in Guntur offering state-of-the-art medical care. With over 20 years of experience, we are dedicated to delivering personalized treatment and comprehensive healthcare services for our patients.

Our hospital is equipped with advanced technology and expert doctors in various specialties, ensuring top-tier healthcare services. From diagnostics to treatment, we provide a complete range of medical services to meet all your health needs.

At Dr. Seshaiah's Praja Vydyasala, patient care is our top priority, and we strive for excellence in every department. We are committed to offering high-quality, accessible healthcare services to the community of Guntur and beyond.
