Welcome to the Surgical Oncology Department at Dr. Seshaiah Prajavydyasala Hospital

At our Surgical Oncology Department, we specialize in the diagnosis and surgical treatment of various types of cancer. Our dedicated team of surgeons, oncologists, and healthcare professionals is committed to providing compassionate, multidisciplinary care tailored to meet the individual needs of each patient.

What is Surgical Oncology?

Surgical oncology is a specialized field focused on the surgical management of cancer. Surgical oncologists are trained to perform procedures to remove tumors and surrounding tissue, as well as to diagnose cancer through biopsies. Our team employs the latest techniques and technologies to ensure optimal outcomes for our patients.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of surgical oncology services, including:

  • Tumor Resection: Surgical removal of tumors from various organs, including the breast, lung, gastrointestinal tract, and skin, with the goal of achieving clear margins and minimizing recurrence.
  • Sentinel Node Biopsy: A minimally invasive procedure to assess the first lymph nodes that cancer may spread to, aiding in cancer staging and treatment planning.
  • Laparoscopic Cancer Surgery: Minimally invasive techniques for treating cancers, allowing for quicker recovery and reduced postoperative pain.
  • Palliative Surgery: Surgical interventions aimed at relieving symptoms and improving quality of life for patients with advanced cancer, even if the cancer cannot be cured.
  • Reconstructive Surgery: Post-cancer surgery reconstruction options to restore appearance and function, especially after breast cancer surgery.

Comprehensive Care Approach

Our approach to cancer treatment is multidisciplinary, involving collaboration among various specialists to create a personalized treatment plan for each patient. This includes:

  • Oncology Consultation: Thorough evaluations with medical oncologists to discuss the best treatment options, including chemotherapy and radiation therapy, in conjunction with surgical interventions.
  • Nutritional Support: Guidance from dietitians to ensure optimal nutrition before, during, and after treatment to support healing and recovery.
  • Psychosocial Support: Access to counselors and support groups to address the emotional and psychological aspects of cancer treatment.

Advanced Diagnostic Services

Accurate diagnosis is critical in oncology. Our department is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools, including:

  • Imaging Studies: CT scans and MRI scans to visualize tumors and assess the extent of cancer spread.
  • Biopsies: Various techniques to obtain tissue samples for pathological examination, helping confirm diagnosis and guide treatment decisions.

Patient Education and Resources

We believe that informed patients make the best decisions regarding their care. Our educational resources include:

  • FAQs: Answers to common questions about surgical oncology procedures and cancer management.
  • Pre- and Post-Operative Guidelines: Essential instructions to help patients prepare for surgery and manage recovery effectively.
  • Support Resources: Information on connecting with support groups, educational materials, and community resources for ongoing support.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to schedule a consultation, please reach out to us. Our dedicated team is here to provide expert care in surgical oncology, helping you navigate your cancer journey with compassion and expertise.
