Meet Our Experts

Dr. N. Seshaiah M.S.

Chairman & Managing Director, Senior General Surgeon & Laparoscopic Surgeon, Ex. Professor & Head, Dept. of Surgery, Govt. General Hospital, Guntur

Biography

Dr. N. Seshaiah, born into a poor farmer's family in the small village of Kovurupalem near Nellore, pursued free education in government medical colleges. He served as a Professor and Head of the Surgery Department at Guntur General Hospital for 30 years, gaining extensive expertise. After voluntarily retiring, he started this hospital on August 15, 1988, with just 5 rupees as the consultation fee in a small room. Over time, the hospital has grown into a five-story building, offering top-notch services at very affordable rates, making it a unique institution.

Meet Our Experts

Dr. G. Saila Bala M.D.

Chairman & Managing Director, Senior General Surgeon & Laparoscopic Surgeon, Ex. Professor & Head, Dept. of Surgery, Govt. General Hospital, Guntur

Biography

Dr. G. Saila Bala, M.D., the wife of Dr. N. Seshaiah, served as a Professor, Head of the Department, Principal of Guntur Medical College, and Superintendent of Guntur Government Hospital for 37 years. She also served as the Head of the Department at Siddhartha Medical College. After retiring, she now oversees the operations of Prajavaidya Sala.

Meet Our Experts

Dr. N. Pavan Kumar M.S., MCh.

Chairman & Managing Director, Senior General Surgeon & Laparoscopic Surgeon, Ex. Professor & Head, Dept. of Surgery, Govt. General Hospital, Guntur

Biography

Dr. N. Pavan Kumar, the son of Dr. N. Seshaiah, completed his M.B.B.S. at the prestigious Bharati Vidyapeeth in Pune and his M.S. at Kamineni Medical College. He further pursued a super-specialty in MCh Paediatric Surgery at JIPMER, one of the world’s most renowned medical colleges, gaining significant experience. He is currently providing full-time medical services at Prajavaidya Sala.

Meet Our Experts

Dr. N. Sindhura M.B.B.S., D.C.D.

Fellowship in Cosmetic Dermatology (UK)

Biography

Dr. N. Sindhura, the wife of Dr. N. Pavan Kumar, completed her M.B.B.S. and pursued further specialization with a Diploma in Clinical Dermatology from the UK. With her expertise in cosmetic dermatology, she has gained extensive experience in advanced skincare and aesthetic treatments. Currently, Dr. Sindhura provides full-time dermatological services at Prajavaidya Sala Multispeciality Hospital, offering cutting-edge solutions for various skin conditions and cosmetic needs.

Dr. S. Siva Sankaraiah M.S. Ortho (NIMS, Hyd), MCh.

Chairman & Managing Director, Senior General Surgeon & Laparoscopic Surgeon, Ex. Professor & Head, Dept. of Surgery, Govt. General Hospital, Guntur

Biography

Dr. S. Siva Sankaraiah, the husband of Dr. N. Niharika (daughter of Dr. N.
